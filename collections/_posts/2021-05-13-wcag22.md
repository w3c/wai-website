---
title: "For Wide Review: WCAG 2.2 Working Draft"
date: 2021-05-13
---

<p><a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/">WCAG 2.2 Working Draft</a> is ready wide review before finalizing WCAG 2.2. This draft has 9 new &ldquo;success criteria&rdquo; (requirements) since WCAG 2.1. The new success criteria and changes since the previous 2.2 draft are introduced in <a href="https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/new-in-22/">What's New in WCAG 2.2</a>. The new success criteria address user needs of people with cognitive or learning disabilities, users of mobile devices, and users of ebooks.   Please submit comments by 11 June 2021.</p>
